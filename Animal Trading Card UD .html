TIGER

Tigers have soft toe pads which allow for them to walk silently through their habitat.

  • Life Span:Tigers have a life span of about 25 years.

  • Fast: Tigers can run over 60 kilometers per hour.

  • Hunting: Tigers prefer to hunt by ambush.

  • Swimming: Tigers are great swimmers.

Tigers typically don't see humans as prey. It is true no tiger has the same stripes. When a tiger roars it can be heard from 3 kilometres away.
